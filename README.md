## So What Even Is Scrivener?

Scrivener is writing software made by Literature & Latte, a small UK company that's been quietly building it since 2007. It's not a word processor exactly — it's more like a writing *environment*. Instead of one giant scrolling document, your manuscript lives in pieces: scenes, chapters, research notes, character sheets, all organized in a sidebar called the Binder. You write each piece in whatever order inspiration hits, and stitch everything together when you're ready to export.

Sounds like a small thing. It genuinely changes how you work.

## What Scrivener Actually Does Well

**The Binder**: This is the heart of it. Every piece of your project — chapter 1, chapter 2, that scene you wrote at 2am that might be chapter 15, your research on 1920s Berlin, your character notes — lives in a sidebar you can navigate instantly. No more hunting through one endless document.

**The Corkboard**: Virtual index cards. Move them around to restructure your plot, and the manuscript moves with them. Writers who outline love this. Writers who don't outline also love it, because you can use it to find structure after the fact.

**The Outliner**: A spreadsheet-style view of your whole manuscript — word counts per section, status labels, synopsis — all at a glance.

**Scrivenings mode**: Flip between editing one scene at a time and editing your whole manuscript as if it were one document. The transition is instant.

**Compile**: When you're done, Scrivener stitches everything together and exports to Word, PDF, ePub, Final Draft, RTF, and more. You can write in your preferred font and export in a completely different format for submission guidelines. This alone saves a lot of formatting headaches.

**Snapshots**: Before a big revision, take a snapshot of a section. If you hate what you did, revert to the earlier version. Compare the before and after to see exactly what changed.

**Sync across devices**: Scrivener is available for macOS, Windows, and iOS. Sync via Dropbox and you can switch between your laptop and iPad mid-session.
